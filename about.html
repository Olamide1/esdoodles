about

hello.

I'm E.S.Doodles — a writer, illustrator, and social media manager drawn to stories about mythology, identity, and the natural world.

My work lives at the crossover between image and text. I've written essays, illustrated comics, managed social media for brands, and built interactive pieces in Rive. The throughline is storytelling — finding the right form for a story, whether that's a caption, a comic page, or a Rive state machine.

I care about craft. The word that lands right, the line that earns its place, the caption that makes someone pause mid-scroll. I take that seriously across every medium.

When I'm not working, I'm reading myth retellings, doing reference studies from botanical illustrations, or mapping out new Rive state machines that will definitely become a side project someday.

working style
  • I work best with clear briefs and room to ask questions early — before the work starts, not during delivery.
  • I communicate often. No surprises at handoff.
  • I take revision rounds seriously and document every decision. You'll always know why something looks the way it does.
  • I genuinely like collaborative feedback processes — good critique makes the work better.
  • I'm a strong self-editor and I'll tell you when something isn't working, even if it was my idea.
