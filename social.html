content calendar — sample week

A typical week's planning structure for a botanical lifestyle brand. Format, pillar, topic, and copy notes — all mapped before content creation begins.

Day Format Pillar Topic Caption note
Monday Carousel (10 slides) Product education 5 ways to style dried botanicals at home Educational, warm, ends with explicit save prompt
Tuesday Story sequence (5 frames) Behind-the-scenes Morning in the studio — audience poll on next collection colour Casual, founder voice, includes poll sticker
Wednesday Single image Seasonal inspiration Current window display — seasonal arrangement Short, atmospheric, no hard CTA
Thursday Carousel (8 slides) Sustainability values How we source — supplier origin stories with specific detail Specific, cited sourcing info — trust-building
Friday Single image + Story Community feature Customer reshare + DM prompt for next week's community feature Warm, community-first, participation CTA
Saturday Story only Product (soft) Weekend moment — product mentioned in lifestyle context Soft sell, lifestyle framing, no price mention
Sunday Rest / optional — No post scheduled — intentional rest day for account Quality over volume. Sustainable consistency > daily pressure.
